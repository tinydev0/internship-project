Refunds

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all electric bike purchases. If you are not satisfied with your bike for any reason, you may return it within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, minus any shipping costs. The bike must be in like-new condition with all original packaging and accessories included For returns, please contact our customer service team to initiate the process and receive a return authorization (RA) number. Returns without an RA number will not be accepted.
Once we receive your returned bike and verify its condition, we will issue a refund to your original method of payment within 7-10 business days.
Please note that any damage caused by misuse or failure to follow maintenance instructions may void the refund eligibility. If you have any questions about our refund policy, feel free to contact us.
